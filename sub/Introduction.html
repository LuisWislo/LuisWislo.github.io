Introduction

We will answer the unknowns of the problem in the question form 5 w + 1 (What, Who, When, Where, Why, How):


  • What is the problem? The problem is the excess of traffic in the city of Guadalajara.
  • Who is the problem? The people who use the car are the main responsibles for the traffic.
  • When is the problem? The problem has been going on for several years now and is on the rise since every year there are more cars in circulation.
  • Where is the problem? The problem is throughout the Metropolitan Area of Guadalajara (ZMG).
  • Why is the problem? The reason behind the problem is, that, in a city like Guadalajara, is common to find traffic, being one of the largest cities in the country, some causes of traffic are: streets in poor condition, many cars at the same time, accidents, the exponential growth of the city, the centralization of the city, etc.
  • How is the problem? The traffic of Guadalajara is something common for those who live there, so they always take into account the time it will take to get to your destination plus the time it takes to move, also the routes you will use as several routes are congested and it is better to have alternative routes if necessary.
