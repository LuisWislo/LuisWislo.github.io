  • Promote public transport
  • Promote the use of the bicycle
  • Use vehicles specially adapted for use in the city
  • Share the car
  • Improve the infrastructure of the streets
  • Redesign sites for bus stops
  • Create underground

These options seem good in general, but let's land a viable solution. If you haven't checked out our interviews with experts on the subject please click here.

Thanks to Carlos Lopez Zaragoza, head of "Unidad de Movilidad No Motorizada y Accesibilidad Universal de Zapopan" who provided us with relevant information on the matter. We have put together these actions that are actually part of Carlos' cause for a better quality of life in the city.

We present to you, our solution...

Sustainable Mobility



Cars certainly make our lives a lot easier, but fully depending on it can be harmful in many ways, not to mention that there are alternatives, depending on the situation, that are substantially better than this motorized vehicle. Our objective is to create a reform based on these principals:

  • Reduce the abuse of the car by taking other alternatives when possible.
  • Fully-supported city infrastructure for bikes.
  • Fight the negative effects of the sedentary lifestyle.
  • Increase the quality of life of citizens in a metropolitan area.
  • Educate people on how to respect and adequately make use of bike and pedestrian infrastructure.


The Cyclist infrastructure

Learn about the power of the bike:
  • filter_dramaBike Lanes
    Make sure that every street has a bike-line on pavement or on the sidewalk with proper maintenance. Bikes will be treated equally and given the same priority as cars.
  • filter_dramaParking spaces for bikes
    Creating more parking spaces for cars will only bring in more cars and saturate the streets. There shall be parking spots for bikes just like there are for cars in every public space.
  • filter_dramaTraining courses
    Many people fear that they will be at a greater risk if they use a bike on the streets. It is all about learning, just like you learned to drive, people will be taught how to properly interact with cars (making turn signals, how to make turns, etc.).
  • filter_dramaYou can do more than you think
    Just take a look at the benefits of using the bike whenever possible:
    • Going to the store around the corner is actually faster by bike.
    • Better physical health and mood!
    • Less polution

Don't forget about walking!


According to a 2015 study by INEGI, 40% of trips to schools and workplaces were made by walking.
Trips made with cars accounted for less than 30%. This means that a very small fraction our occupations make up for a very big problem. How about we stop abusing the poor car?
On 2007, the latest survey of source and destiny was made in the metropolitan city of Guadalajara, and it was discovered that 40% of trips with cars were of 3KM or less. A distance easily achievable with a bike (or wakling, if you have the time).

Safe Streets


After all, both cars and bikes need to coexist. To do so, the streets need to pass some quality testing so both indiviudal and public means of transport can move successfully from one place to another.
Movilidad de Zapopan is currently working with statistical information that, after being analyzed, gives them valuable insight on where certain infrastructure needs to be updated (traffic lights, signs, bus stops, etc.).

The Cultural Shift

We've seen that using a more sustainable means of transport when possible can increase your quality of life. However, we are so used to depend on the car, that not many people take the time to think twice about what they are doing to their health.

Please help us spread the message. We can actually make people's daily lives less stressful, leading to all kinds of benefits to the body and mind. By supporting our reform and the work form Movilidad de Zapopan we will make a change!
