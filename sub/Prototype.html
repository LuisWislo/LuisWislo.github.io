User Feedback

About our solution, we received the following feedback:
  • Concern on the space of the streets or sidewalks if bike-lanes become mandatory (it just wouldn't work for all streets).
  • Lack of bicycle parking spots (making you less prone to going to some places by bike).
  • Pedestrians and drivers not respecting the bike-lanes.
  • Increase of accidents as more people will be active on the streets.
