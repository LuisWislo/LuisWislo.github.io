Traffic congestion

Bad for everyone

Heavy traffic in big cities is an issue we have to deal with every day. The excess of motorized vehicles keeps us from getting to work in time, leaves a big carbon footprint, stresses our cars’ engines, increases chances of collision, and overwhelms even the best of us. There are other alternatives to cars that are able to lighten the streets, but must people don’t invest on them. What can we do?

Get informed


Introduction

The problem, categorized:

    What is going on?

    The problem is the excess of traffic in the city of Guadalajara and other metropolitan areas.



    Who is responsible for this?

    People who use cars. There is no way around it. If you own and regularly utilize a motorized vehicle, then you are causing traffic. We need to find ways to minimize its usage.



    When did it start?

    Traffic is a staple problem for big and busy cities. The only thing we are certain of is that it on the rise since every day there are new cars on the road.



    Where does it manifest?

    On main roads and speedways, specially during peak hours. Unfortunately, it is sometimes difficult to avoid them.



    Why does it happen?

    The reason behind the problem is, that, in a city like Guadalajara, it is common to find traffic, being one of the largest cities in the country, some causes of traffic are: streets in poor condition, many cars present at the same time, accidents, the exponential growth of the city, the centralization of the city, etc.



    How does it affect people?

    The traffic of Guadalajara is something common for those who live there, so they always take into account the time it will take them to get to their destination considering the possibility of a slowdown. Also, the routes you use as well as many others may be congested so it is better to know about alternative roads depending on your trip.
